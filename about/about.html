Our Mission

Pressure Reliever is designed to help people release pressures that can build up from different sources such as life, school and work. There are 3 main sections on this website to help you: activities, therapy and forums. The Pressure Reliever Community is here for you to share your story and offer you support in a variety of ways.

To help people who are overwhelmed by stress, we provide advocacy, activities, support and public awareness so that all individuals and families affected by stressed minds can build better lives. If you have any questions, please contact us at pressurereliever@outlook.com.
